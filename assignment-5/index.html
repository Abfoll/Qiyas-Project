ABOUT

Adele Laurie Blue Adkins is an English singer-songwriter known for her powerful voice and emotionally direct pop ballads. After graduating from the BRIT School in London, she released her debut album in 2008 and quickly became one of the most successful artists of her generation.

Her records have earned Grammy Awards, an Academy Award, and global acclaim. Songs like Rolling in the Deep and Someone Like You helped define a modern era of pop music while keeping a classic, soulful character.
